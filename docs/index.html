Every knob, deliberate.

Pure Swift over Network.framework. No Electron, no Python, no bundled LLM weights, no third-party Swift deps.

🌐

Multi-interface aggregation

Every outbound socket pinned to a specific NIC via IP_BOUND_IF. Wi-Fi + Ethernet + iPhone tether + Thunderbolt, together, reassembled and verified.

⚡

BitTorrent v1 + v2

Native DHT + magnets + seeding. Every peer connection pinned to the interface you chose — your tether doesn't accidentally sync 40 GB.

📲

Phone remote

QR-pair any phone with this Mac. Paste URLs from Safari's share sheet; watch throughput tick up live on your iPhone.

🔐

Verified bytes

SHA-256 on every completed download. Per-chunk Merkle integrity when a .splynek-manifest sibling exists. BEP 52 throughout.

🏠

LAN cache

Other Splyneks on your network advertise via Bonjour. Your colleague's copy of that 5 GB ISO arrives over gigabit, not the internet.

📡

Per-interface DoH

DNS over HTTPS, per lane, on the same interface the bytes flow through. No DNS leaks across networks, no ISP snooping on your lookups.
