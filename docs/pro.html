What's in the $29 tier

AI Concierge LOCAL

Chat-first control for Splynek. Type "download the latest Firefox," "find that iOS SDK I grabbed in January," or "cancel everything" — the local LLM routes it to the right action. Powered by Ollama on your Mac; no cloud, no round-trip, no data leaves your machine.

Agentic Recipes LOCAL

Type a plain-English goal — "set up my Mac for iOS dev," "mirror the latest Ubuntu release" — and the local LLM proposes a multi-item download plan. Review, uncheck items you don't want, queue the rest in one click.

Scheduled Downloads

Restrict starts to time windows (e.g., overnight on home Wi-Fi), weekdays, and cellular-off rules. Running downloads are never interrupted — the schedule only gates starts. Waiting queue entries show a "WAITING · next opening 2h" pill.

Phone Pairing LAN

Scan a QR code with your phone to pair it to this Mac over the LAN. Submit download URLs from your phone; Splynek does the actual download on your Mac. Your phone stays fast; the download doesn't touch its battery or tether quota.

Pricing rationale

$29 once. Not $5/month. Not $29/year. You pay once and the app keeps working. New features on the 0.x line ship free to existing Pro users.

No enforcement via honour system. v0.33–v0.43 shipped Pro with an HMAC gate and a publicly-readable secret in the source — effectively honour-system. We acknowledged that and fixed it in v0.44/v0.45: the Pro modules are now closed-source and gated by Apple's StoreKit receipt check. The $29 is real.

Free tier still covers 80% of users. If all you want is a download manager that saturates every NIC you've got plus torrents, use the DMG. It's free. Support the dev by starring the repo and telling friends.

Frequently asked

Does Splynek Pro phone home?

No. Like the free tier, every AI feature runs locally via Ollama. The REST API on your LAN only talks to phones you've paired via QR. No analytics, no crash reports, no telemetry. See the privacy policy.

Do I need Ollama?

Only for the two AI features (Concierge + Recipes). Scheduling and Phone Pairing work without Ollama. Ollama is free, one-command install from ollama.com; Splynek auto-detects it.

Is this a subscription?

No. One-time $29, non-consumable Mac App Store In-App Purchase. If you stop updating, what you paid for keeps working forever — Apple doesn't revoke purchased apps.

What if I want a refund?

Request it directly from Apple — App Store → your profile → Purchased → Report a Problem next to Splynek. Apple usually approves refunds within 24 hours.

Will you add a cloud-AI version for folks without Ollama?

No current plans. Local-only is a feature, not a limitation — it keeps your download history off every LLM vendor's training set. If Ollama's install flow is blocking you, tell us via email — we can ship better onboarding.

Do the v0.33–v0.43 HMAC keys still work?

No. The v0.44 split removed HMAC validation; Pro is StoreKit-only from v0.45 forward. If you were a friend/early tester who got an HMAC key and bought the MAS version, email us — we'll waive the $29.

Family Sharing?

Splynek Pro is not Family Share-enabled in v1. One key per Apple ID. We may revisit if asked.
