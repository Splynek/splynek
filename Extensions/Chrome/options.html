(found in Splynek → Settings → Painel web)

DRM streams (Netflix, Disney+, Apple TV+) are skipped automatically — Splynek won't intercept them. Open-HLS streams (YouTube non-Premium, Vimeo, Twitch, Plex) are accelerated.

About

The Accelerator only intercepts. It never makes its own network requests, never reads page DOM (no content scripts), never phones home. Per-host preferences sync across your Chrome installs via chrome.storage.sync.

Bug? See Extensions/Chrome/ACCELERATOR-DESIGN.md in the Splynek repo.
